# StudExEl - Project Vision

## Overview

StudExEl is a secure student-exclusive marketplace designed to connect verified students across universities and colleges in the Philippines.

Unlike traditional online marketplaces, StudExEl focuses on trust, safety, and community by ensuring that only verified students can fully participate in buying, selling, and interacting with other users.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often use public marketplaces to buy and sell:

- Textbooks
- School supplies
- Gadgets
- Dorm essentials
- Uniforms

However, these platforms have several challenges:

- Fake accounts
- Scammers
- No student verification
- Difficult communication
- No campus-focused features

---

## Solution

StudExEl provides a trusted platform where students can safely:

- Buy and sell second-hand items
- Search by school
- Connect with fellow students
- Verify their identity through student credentials
- Build a trusted academic marketplace

---

## Mission

To create the safest and most trusted digital marketplace for students in the Philippines.

---

## Vision

To become the leading student ecosystem that supports commerce, collaboration, and campus life.
